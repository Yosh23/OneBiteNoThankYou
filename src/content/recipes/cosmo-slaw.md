1. **Char the jalapeño.** Hold it with tongs directly over a gas flame, or set it under a broiler, turning until the skin is blackened on all sides. Set aside to cool, then pull off the stem.

2. **Prep the slaw.** Slice the napa cabbage thin. Shave the fennel bulb — a mandoline makes it easy. Julienne the red onion. Grate the carrot. Roughly chop the cilantro.

3. **Make the dressing.** In a blender, combine the mayonnaise, honey, cider vinegar, and the charred jalapeño. Blend until smooth.

4. **Finish the dressing.** Pour into a bowl. Whisk in the poppy seeds and a pinch of salt. Taste and adjust.

5. **Toss.** In a large bowl, combine all the prepared vegetables. Pour the dressing over the top and toss until everything is evenly coated. Taste once more and add salt if needed.
